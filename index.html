Ship & Cargo Brokerage

involves acting as an intermediary between shipowners and those who need to transport goods by sea,In essence, ship and cargo brokers play a crucial role in the maritime industry by ensuring that ships are efficiently utilized and cargo is transported effectively.

Cargo Charterer

A Cargo Charterer is an individual or company that hires a vessel to transport cargo. They may own the cargo themselves or act on behalf of other parties needing transportation services. The charterer negotiates terms with the shipowner, including the freight rate, duration, and specific conditions of the transport.

Ship's Sell & Purchase

The Ship Sale and Purchase (S&P) market is a specialized sector within the maritime industry where ships are bought and sold. This market is crucial for the trade, exchange, and reallocation of marine vessels among various shipping companies and operators.

BIMCO Contracts

BIMCO Contracts are standardized agreements widely used in the shipping industry. Developed by the Baltic and International Maritime Council (BIMCO), these contracts cover various aspects of maritime activities, including charter parties, bills of lading, and other standard agreements.

Survey & Inspection

A survey involves a detailed assessment of a ship or its cargo to determine its condition, value, and compliance with regulations & Inspections are more focused examinations to ensure that specific standards and regulations are met. They often involve checking for defects, safety compliance, and operational readiness.
